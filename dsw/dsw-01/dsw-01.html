Body text. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. This is a paragraph of prose set in Instrument Sans at 15px with a line-height of 1.55 and a max-width of 62ch to maintain an optimal reading measure. Tokens govern every value. Inline links use the link color token with underline offset.

Markets can remain irrational longer than you can remain solvent. Design systems can drift longer than you can remain consistent without one. Both require discipline enforced by structure rather than goodwill.

Ordered and unordered lists with consistent indentation and item rhythm:

  1. Tokens define values — color, type, space, motion
  2. Element styles apply tokens to naked HTML
  3. Layout primitives control spatial composition
  4. Components encapsulate behavior and anatomy
  • OKLCH perceptual colorspace for all color operations
  • APCA contrast algorithm for all legibility decisions
  • CSS @layer for predictable cascade without specificity games
  • Custom Elements as optional behavioral layer

What is APCA?

The Advanced Perceptual Contrast Algorithm (APCA) is a modern approach to measuring contrast that accounts for spatial frequency, viewing conditions, and the non-linearity of human visual perception. It produces Lc values (perceptual lightness contrast) that more accurately predict readability than the WCAG 2.x relative luminance ratio.

What is total return?

Total return includes both capital gains (price appreciation) and income (dividends or interest). It represents the actual gain or loss of an investment over time.

How is risk calculated?

Risk is measured using standard deviation of historical returns and beta relative to the benchmark index. Higher values indicate greater volatility.

What is rebalancing?

Rebalancing is the process of realigning the weightings of a portfolio to maintain the original desired level of asset allocation or risk.
